DELHI

        
Delhi, the bustling capital city of India, is a vibrant metropolis that seamlessly weaves together a rich tapestry of history, culture, and modernity.
 As one of the world's oldest continually inhabited cities, Delhi is a treasure trove of architectural marvels, diverse traditions, and a myriad of flavors.
 From the ancient lanes of Old Delhi, adorned with Mughal-era gems, to the cosmopolitan avenues of New Delhi,
 where government institutions stand tall, the city offers a captivating blend of the old and the new.
