TRIVIA TIDBITS

    
        

            





        
 *	Historical Significance: Delhi is one of the oldest cities in the world, with a history dating back over 2,500 years.
 It has been the capital of several empires, including the Maurya, Mughal, and British Empires.

        
 *	Multiple Capitals: Delhi is unique in having been the capital of both ancient and modern India.
 It served as the capital of various Indian empires and later became the capital of the Republic of India in 1950.

        
 *	Diverse Culture: Delhi is a melting pot of cultures and religions. It's known for its diverse population,
 which includes people from all over India and the world, resulting in a rich cultural tapestry.

        
 *   UNESCO World Heritage Sites: Delhi boasts several UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the Red Fort,
 Qutub Minar, and Humayun's Tomb, showcasing its historical and architectural significance.

        
 *	Street Food Paradise: Delhi is famous for its street food, offering a wide variety of delicious snacks
 and dishes like chaat, kebabs, and parathas. Old Delhi, in particular, is a food lover's paradise.

        
 *	Political Hub: As the capital of India, Delhi is the center of political power in the country.
 It houses the Indian Parliament, the President's Residence (Rashtrapati Bhavan), and the Supreme Court.

        
 *	Educational Hub: Delhi is home to some of India's most prestigious educational institutions,
 including the University of Delhi, Jawaharlal Nehru University, and the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi.
