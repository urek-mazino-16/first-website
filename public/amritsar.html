AMRITSAR

        
Amritsar, the spiritual heart of Sikhism, is a city with a soul that resonates with faith, devotion, and history. Located in the
 northern Indian state of Punjab, Amritsar is most renowned for the exquisite Golden Temple, a place of worship and tranquility that attracts millions
 of pilgrims and visitors from around the world. Beyond its spiritual significance, Amritsar is also celebrated
 for its rich culinary heritage, particularly its mouthwatering Punjabi cuisine. The city's history is
 deeply intertwined with the struggles of India's independence, making it a place where both spirituality and history come to life.
